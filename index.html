World-Class Teacher Training

The Cambridge CELTA — the gold standard in English language teaching

The Certificate in English Language Teaching to Adults (CELTA) is awarded by Cambridge Assessment English — one of the world's most respected educational institutions. It is recognised and respected by employers in over 100 countries.

What Is CELTA?

A qualification built for real classrooms

CELTA is not just a theoretical course — it combines practical teaching experience with evidence-based methodology. You'll plan and deliver real lessons to real adult learners, observed and guided by experienced tutors throughout.

It is one of the only entry-level qualifications in English language teaching that requires candidates to complete observed, assessed teaching practice as part of the course.

  • Awarded by Cambridge Assessment English
  • Recognised in 100+ countries worldwide
  • Practical teaching practice built into the course
  • No prior teaching experience required
  • Suitable for recent graduates and career changers

Inside the Course

Four pillars of expert teaching

🧠
Language Analysis
Deep understanding of English grammar, phonology, and vocabulary — so you can explain it clearly to learners at any level.
📝
Lesson Planning
Design engaging, learner-centred lessons using proven frameworks. From warmer activities to assessment, every stage has purpose.
🎤
Teaching Practice
Observed, assessed lessons with real adult learners. You'll teach, receive expert feedback, and grow with every session.
📚
Professional Skills
Learner needs analysis, error correction techniques, classroom management, and using materials effectively in diverse contexts.
What You'll Gain

Life after CELTA — what to expect

A CELTA qualification doesn't just open doors — it builds the foundation for a fulfilling, flexible, and internationally mobile career in education.

✈️
Teach Anywhere in the World
CELTA is recognised by language schools, universities, and English-medium institutions across more than 100 countries. Whether you dream of Tokyo, Madrid, São Paulo, or Dubai — your certificate travels with you.
💼
Stand Out to Employers
CELTA holders are preferred candidates at reputable language schools worldwide. Many employers specifically require or prioritise Cambridge-accredited qualifications when recruiting international teaching staff.
🌱
Build a Career with Purpose
Teaching English is one of the most rewarding careers you can have. You'll change lives — helping students access opportunities, careers, and connections that only language unlocks.
📈
A Springboard for Growth
CELTA is recognised as an entry point to further Cambridge qualifications like DELTA (Diploma in English Language Teaching). As you grow in experience, your career and earning potential grow with you.
🤝
Join a Global Community
You'll graduate into a worldwide community of CELTA-trained teachers. Through Orbit's alumni network, you'll stay connected, supported, and inspired throughout your teaching journey.
🧳
Freedom & Flexibility
Design the life you want. Teach full-time, part-time, or freelance. Work in classrooms, online, or in corporate settings. CELTA gives you the credential — you choose the lifestyle.
